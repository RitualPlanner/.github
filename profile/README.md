# About RitualPlanner

RitualPlanner is a modern SaaS platform designed to simplify the day-to-day work of priests by bringing traditional ritual management into the digital era.

Instead of relying on physical diaries, phone calls, and scattered records, RitualPlanner provides one centralized platform for managing rituals, clients, schedules, finances, reminders, and collaboration.

Our goal is simple:

> Help priests spend more time performing rituals and less time managing paperwork.

---

# Why RitualPlanner?

Traditional priest management often involves:

- Physical notebooks
- Manual scheduling
- Phone call coordination
- Payment tracking by hand
- No centralized client history

RitualPlanner solves these problems through a secure, modern, and easy-to-use platform built specifically for priests.
